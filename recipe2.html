Pancake

Soft, fluffy and easy breakfast recipe

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Baking powder

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, milk, and egg together
  2. Stir until smooth
  3. Heat pan and add butter
  4. Pour mixture into pan
  5. Cook both sides until golden brown
  6. Serve hot with syrup or honey
