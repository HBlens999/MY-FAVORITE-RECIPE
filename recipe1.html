Jollof Rice

A popular West African dish full of flavor

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Pepper
  • Onions
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Wash the rice properly
  2. Blend tomatoes, pepper, and onions
  3. Fry the mixture with oil
  4. Add seasoning and stock
  5. Add rice and cook until soft
